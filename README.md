Fireside mod by Sam S.

How to Use:

This mod allows you to sit on logs that have two blocks of air above them by using them with an empty hand. It also 
allows you to skip time without setting your respawn point. To do this, it adds two keybinds to the game.

Select Fireside Duration (defaults to backslash): When you use this key, you cycle through the times of day that you
could skip to: midnight, morning, noon, night, and clear weather

Submit Fireside Duration (defaults to enter): When you use this key, if you are sitting on a log with a campfire two
blocks away (similar spacing to bookshelves), you will wait on that log until you get up or the selected time occurs. 
If all players are sleeping or waiting by campfires, the time will skip to the nearest selected time of day.

Weather:

When it is raining, campfires exposed to the rain may be randomly extinguished. Soul campfires and campfires with high
smoke (such as by placing hay underneath) do not get extinguished in rain. When trying to skip time at a campfire while
it is raining, if anyone's campfire is liable to going out, a random player whose campfire might go out will be
extinguished and time will not successfully be skipped.

If it is raining and all players are sleeping or waiting at campfires until clear weather, assuming nobody's campfire 
is liable to going out, the time will be set to day and the weather cycle will be cleared.
